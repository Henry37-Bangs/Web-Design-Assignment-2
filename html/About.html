ABOUT US

VISION

The Sierra Leone Football Agency envisions a vibrant football culture that nurtures talent,
promotes professionalism, and unites communities. Its goal is to develop local players,
enhance league standards, and position Sierra Leone as a competitive football nation, both in
Africa and globally, through sustainable growth and inclusive opportunities.

MISSION

The mission of the Sierra Leone Football Agency is to develop, regulate, and promote football
nationwide by investing in grassroots programs, empowering young talent, enhancing
infrastructure, and fostering partnerships. It strives to build a transparent, inclusive,
competitive football environment that elevates national pride and international recognition.
