SIERRA LEONE FOOTBALL AGENCY

NEWS

The Sierra Leone Football Association continues to enhance football development nationwide by promoting grassroots programs,

strengthening league structures, and supporting national team participation. Recent initiatives focus on youth engagement,

infrastructure improvement, and international collaboration, aiming

to uplift the sport’s standards and foster a stronger football culture across the country.

INTRODUCTION

The Sierra Leone Football Agency (SLFA) serves as the governing body responsible for overseeing football activities in Sierra Leone. It is dedicated

to promoting the development, regulation, and growth of football at all levels across the country—from grassroots to professional leagues. The

agency manages national teams, organizes domestic competitions, and partners with international bodies like FIFA and CAF to ensure

global standards are met. By fostering talent and creating opportunities for young athletes, the SLFA aims to elevate Sierra Leone's presence in

global football. Its mission also includes maintaining integrity, transparency, and professionalism in all aspects of the sport.
